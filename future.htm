Speculative Timelines · Post-2025

Mapping futures from 2025 to 3000 AD (and a bit beyond).

This page is a hub for long-range scenarios, branching timelines, and “what if” experiments starting after 2025. Some are grounded in geopolitics and religion, others in Skynet-level AI or alien contact. Together they sketch possible paths from our present into war, peace, federation, and a fully occupied Solar System.

How to use this page

Each card below links to a dedicated scenario page:

  • World War III and Skynet style tech-escalation paths.
  • Alien contact – cooperative and hostile variants.
  • AI futures centred on the Technological Singularity.
  • Bahá’í-informed futures focused on peace and world order.
  • Solar system maps of life in 3000 AD.
