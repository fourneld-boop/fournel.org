Personal Wormhole · Dan Fournel

Hi, I’m Dan – this is my wormhole of ideas.

I’m a SF and history buff based in British Columbia, working at the intersection of technology, small-scale experiments, long-range future scenarios, religious study, and deep-time historical timelines. This site collects projects, notes, and speculative worlds I’m currently exploring.

What you’ll find here

Think of fournel.org as a personal control room for alternate histories and futures:

  • History & religion: long sweeps from 10,000 BC to now, including a dedicated religious timeline.
  • Technology: a deep-time view from stone tools to far-future capabilities in tech.htm.
  • Scenarios: structured futures like World War III, Skynet 2027, Alien Contact 2026, Alien Attack 2026, and Bahai-inspired world order.
  • About & meta: how and why these timelines were put together in about.htm.
